Games


The Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama incorporates games into all classes. Students are encouraged to express themselves and participate which can be a wonderful opportunity to discover leadership skills and proves time and time again to be an effective team builder. Overall students find game playing to be great fun which is one of our main goals at the Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama.

Games feature strongly with dramatic training. Games can be used in many situations such as

  • Warm ups
  • Establishing leadership skills
  • Working both alone and in groups
  • Encouraging co-operation
  • Development of language skills
  • Creativity and
  • Confidence

A great deal can be learnt through games whilst in a fun environment.
