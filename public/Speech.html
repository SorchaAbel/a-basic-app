Speech


Good speech is important in drama for both exams and dramatic performances. Good speech helps to build confidence for students speaking in public and also helps develop important social skills. The Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama incorporates speech into all classes.

Speech lessons ensure that each child develops a greater understanding and an increased degree of confidence when performing or speaking. They also help correct basic speech faults, pronunciation and help to develop a full speaking voice with the ai of vocal and breathing exercises. Emphasis is placed on voice and voice projection through activities involving breathing, articulation and projection.

Lessons will include a range of strategies including;

  • Vocal exercises,
  • Theatrical games and
  • Performance activities.

Fiona’s objective is to enhance a student's ability to communicate in a variety of contexts and circumstances, including preparing and delivering speeches, interactive speaking, story telling, initiating conversation and social speaking. Students are encouraged to express themselves which can be a wonderful opportunity to discover hidden talents and proves time and time again to be an effective team builder. Overall students find speech to be great fun and challenging which is one of our main goals at the Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama.
