Movement to Music


Similar to mime in many of its values, movement to music is an excellent way to develop the use of the body, gesture, expressing of emotion and of course concentration. The movement is accompanied by music and requires sensitivity, imagination and observations.

The Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama incorporates movement to music into all classes. Students are encouraged to express themselves which can be a wonderful opportunity to discover hidden talents and proves time and time again to be an effective team builder. Overall students find movement to music to be great fun which is one our of our main goals at the Fiona Keane School of Speech and Drama.

Overall the Fiona Keane School of Speech & Drama understands that everyone is different and as such classes are designed to enhance personalities, overcome shyness and improve students overall speech and diction. All classes are designed with two primary goals; to be enjoyable and fun!
