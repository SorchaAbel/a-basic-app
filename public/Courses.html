Courses

Classes are divided into 3 separate groups depending on the students ages. Typical break down of ages is;

  1. 4-6 yrs
  2. 7-9 yrs and
  3. 9-14 yrs.

The year is broken down in to 3 terms each of approx 9 wks in length and running between September and May every year.

During Terms 1 and 2 we encourage a lot of confidence building fun and games exercises, we study mime, movement to music, improvisation, poetry, speech and acting. We prepare for our annual exams during term 3, which normally take place in May each year and are in association with the Irish Board of Speech & Drama. www.irishboard.ie

Exams are optional but are encouraged, as they are a wonderful exercise in building up confidence in children and young adults and also giving the individuals in the classes a goal for the end of year and a sense of achievement.
