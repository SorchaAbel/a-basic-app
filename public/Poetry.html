Poetry


Poetry is explored for both the love and appreciation of it. It is also important to have some knowledge of poetry for examinations.

Learning poetry can be important in the sense that it allows the students to explore their inner most thoughts and feelings and also project them in ways they may not have known were possible.

At Fiona Keane's School we endeavour to teach children all different kinds of drama and different ways of expressing themselves. Poetry, especially when combined with speech courses will give students the opportunity to deliver poems in front of their peers with sensitivity, feeling and confidence. Most importantly the students can have a lot of fun.
